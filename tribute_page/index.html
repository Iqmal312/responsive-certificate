Tun Abdullah Ahmad Badawi

About Pak Lah:

  • Born: November 26, 1939 in Bayan Lepas, Penang
  • Education: Graduated from University of Malaya in Islamic Studies
  • Political Career: Joined civil service in 1964, entered politics in 1978
  • Ministerial Positions: Held various portfolios including Education and Foreign Affairs
  • Prime Minister: Served from October 31, 2003 to April 3, 2009
  • Nickname: Known affectionately as "Pak Lah"
  • Islam Hadhari: Introduced this progressive Islamic concept
  • Retirement: Stepped down in 2009, succeeded by Najib Razak

Pak Lah is remembered for his moderate leadership style and efforts to fight corruption.
